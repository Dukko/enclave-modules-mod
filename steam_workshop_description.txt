[h1]Drukhal's Enclave Additions[/h1]

Adds starbase buildings and waystation modules for the enclaves that never got them. Each enclave unlocks content via a partnership event once your opinion reaches 50+.

[h2]What's Included[/h2]

[b]Shroudwalkers[/b] — Shroud Conduit (building) / Shroud Communion Chamber (waystation). Unity and tradition cost bonuses; psionic empires get deeper rewards including Zro production.

[b]Mindwardens[/b] — Warden's Vigil (building) / Warden's Monitoring Post (waystation). Cloaking detection, ship upkeep reduction, and spy network bonuses. Not available to psionic empires.

[b]Curators[/b] — Curator Archive (building) / Curator Survey Terminal (waystation). Physics and society research, anomaly generation bonuses. [i]Requires Leviathans DLC.[/i]

[b]Artisan Troubadours[/b] — Artisan Stage (building) / Artisan Touring Stage (waystation). Unity, pop happiness, and amenity bonuses. Spiritualist empires get extra rewards. [i]Requires Leviathans DLC.[/i]

[b]Trader Enclaves (XuraCorp, Riggan, Muutagan)[/b] — One building and one waystation module each, producing their respective strategic resource. If all three like you simultaneously, they'll offer [b]The Traders' Compact[/b] — all three partnerships at a discount.

[b]Caravaneers[/b] — Caravaneer Credit Exchange (building) / Caravaneer Waypoint Exchange (waystation). Trade, empire size, and trade fee reductions. Not available to gestalt empires. [i]Requires MegaCorp DLC.[/i]

[h2]Grand Commercial Network[/h2]

Hold all three Trader Compact partnerships [i]and[/i] the Caravaneer partnership and the four commercial enclaves will propose a unified economic framework. One-time permanent bonus: 1000 energy + 200 alloys + rare resources, plus empire-wide -10% trade fee, -5% empire size, -3% ship upkeep. [i]Requires MegaCorp DLC.[/i]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
[b]Overlord[/b] DLC required for waystation modules. [b]Leviathans[/b] and [b]MegaCorp[/b] DLC for respective content. Does not overwrite any vanilla files.
