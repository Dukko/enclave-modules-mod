# Drukhal's Enclave Additions

A Stellaris mod that adds starbase buildings and waystation modules for the enclaves that never got them. Each enclave unlocks content through a partnership event once your opinion reaches 50+.

**[Steam Workshop](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3746975952)**

---

## What's Included

Each enclave provides two pieces of content: a **starbase building** for traditional (settled) empires, and a **waystation module** for nomadic empires. All are one-per-empire.

---

### Shroudwalkers

**Shroud Conduit** *(starbase building, 750 energy, 3 upkeep)*
- +5 unity (all empires)
- +5 unity, +5 society research *(psionic empires)*
- +3 physics, +2 society research *(non-psionic empires)*
- +3 unity *(spiritualist empires)*
- +1 Zro *(psionic empires with Zro mining tech)*
- Country: −5% tradition cost
- Country: −3% tradition cost, +5% unity from jobs *(spiritualist)*

**Shroud Communion Chamber** *(waystation module, 100 alloys, 2 trade upkeep)*
- Station: +10 unity per system
- Station: +5 unity, +5 society research per system *(psionic)*
- Station: +3 physics, +2 society research per system *(non-psionic)*
- Station: +1 Zro per system *(psionic + Zro mining tech)*
- Wayline network: −3% tradition cost, +2% research sharing

## Special Events

**The Traders' Compact** — fires when XuraCorp, Riggan, and Muutagan all reach opinion 50+ simultaneously. Unlocks all three trader partnerships at a bundled discount instead of paying for each separately.

**Grand Commercial Network** — requires all three trader partnerships *and* the Caravaneer partnership. Permanent one-time bonus: 1000 energy, 200 alloys, rare resources, plus empire-wide −10% trade fee, −5% empire size, −3% ship upkeep. *(MegaCorp DLC)*

---

## Design Notes

- Each building and module is **limited to one per empire**. The option hides once one is built anywhere in your empire.
- Wayline network modifiers are empire-wide bonuses from the partnership — they don't require the enclave present in-system.
- Sacred path bonuses (Joyous Enlightenment, Middle Path) are integrated into all waystation modules.
